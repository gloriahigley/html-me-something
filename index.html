About

Hello, and welcome to my website. My name is Gloria Higley and I am an aspiring software developer.

I am currently a Knowledge Manager with a large financial technology company in Kansas City, MO. I love my job and am learning to code to enhance my skillset for my current role and eventually hope to transition to a more technical job.

I have been learning how to code for the past two years via a mixture of online tutorials, local workshops, and am now currently enrolled in LaunchCode 101. The support in Kansas City for learning to code in phenomenal, especially for those under represented in tech.

When I'm not learning to code you can find me at the lake with my husband, Andrew and our daughter, Fiona.
